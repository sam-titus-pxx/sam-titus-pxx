About The Author

Fatherly P. Titus

Chemical Engineer
Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria

Fatherly Titus is a medium-scale student farmer based in the heart of Uyo Metropolis, Akwa Ibom State.

He is currently in the university doing a 5-years course in Chemical and Process Engineering at the University of Uyo, Akwa Ibom State.

He has 5+ years experience in farming and agro-production in the tropical South-South region of Nigeria where he engages in cultivating crops that are native and indigenous to the geopolitical area.

He specializes in growing Corn, Peppers, Cassava, Fluted Pumpkin (Vegetable), Sweet Potatoes, Spice Herbs (Scent Leaf and Curry), and Bitter Leaf.
